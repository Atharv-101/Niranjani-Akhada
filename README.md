# Niranjani-Akhada
Shri Panchayati Akhara Niranjani is one of the most prominent, ancient monastic orders of Hinduism. Tracing its origins to 804 AD in Mandvi, Gujarat, it is headquartered in Haridwar, Uttarakhand. Known for its scholarly sadhus, the akhara has a significant presence at the Kumbh Mela and includes many highly educated professionals.
